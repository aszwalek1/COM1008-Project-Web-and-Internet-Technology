WHAT WE DO

Rivia is a game development company creating games for people in every age. We create role-playing games, simulations, puzzles and many more. Our newest goal is creating web-based games for children. We strongly believe child's development is very important and we want to help parents in this difficult task.

SUSTAINABILITY

In Rivia we strongly believe in sustainable game development. That is why our games are all either web-based or sold online. Our offices are plastic free. We also support many environmental charities and organise charity picnics.
